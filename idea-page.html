Inspiration

My Idea

This website is a collaborative curriculum-planning tool that leverages AI to align academic instruction with professional demand. By parsing job descriptions and student preferences, the platform generates tailored lesson plans and activities that ensure every classroom hour translates into real-world career readiness, all while staying true to the teacher's core course objectives. This app would promote student engagement and improve employability by integrating market-relevant skills into the learning experience.

Key Features

  • AI Recommended Curriculum / Assignments for Teachers:
    Utilizes AI algorithms to analyze job market trends and align course content with in-demand skills.
  • Customizable Lesson Plans:
    Allows educators to create and modify lesson plans that incorporate relevant industry skills while adhering to their syllabus and academic standards.
  • Student Preferences:
    Gathers student interests and career aspirations to tailor learning experiences that resonate with their goals.
  • Login:
    Secure authentication system for educators and students to access their personalized features.

How the App Would Work

To get started, a teacher simply uploads their syllabus as a PDF and clicks "Analyze" to let the AI identify the core goals of the class. Next, the students can paste in a few job descriptions from the industry they want to be ready for. The AI then compares the two, finding the specific skills that are missing from the current plan. At the same time, students log in to a simple dashboard where they click to rank which topics interest them most through a quick series of questions. Once all the information is submitted, the website generates a list of ready-to-use lesson plans, homework assignments, and project ideas that blend the teacher's original goals with real-world career skills and student interests. The teacher can then click to tweak or edit any part of these suggestions to perfectly match their personal teaching style before saving the final version.
